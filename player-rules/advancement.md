# Advancement

No experience points. Characters advance through expeditions and accomplishments.

---

## Leveling Up

ATK, DEF, and save target advance automatically with level. HP: roll your full HD pool at level-up and take the new result if it is higher than your current maximum; otherwise gain +1. RES modifier applies per die rolled.

---

## Expedition Rewards

At the end of each expedition with a significant objective completed, the referee awards one of the following:

| Reward | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| +1 to a d6 task | Specific task or category; must relate to what happened |
| +1 to a stat | Referee determines or player makes a case |
| +1 to a save | Permanent; tied to what was survived |
| New talent | Requires fictional justification |

---

## Level Bands

Advancement halts at each band ceiling until an Accomplishment unlocks the next. Advancing is always optional.

| Levels | Title | Scale |
|--------|-------|-------|
| 1-2 | Adventurer | Unknown. Survives on luck and grit. |
| 3-4 | Hero | Local reputation. |
| 5-6 | Lord | Regional power. |
| 7-8 | Legend | Continental scale. |
| 9-10 | Mythic | Near-godlike. |

---

## Accomplishments

An Accomplishment is a deed of sufficient magnitude to mark a transition in who the character is. The reward is always a choice: unlock the next level band, or take a concrete bonus (+1 to a stat, +1 to all saves of a type, or a significant talent).

**Advancing is always optional.** A character may take the concrete bonus instead of unlocking the next band and remain at their current tier indefinitely.

For examples of deeds that qualify at each transition, see [Rewards & Accomplishments](../campaign-rules/rewards.md).
