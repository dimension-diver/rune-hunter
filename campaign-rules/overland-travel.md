# Overland Travel

Travel is measured in hexes of approximately 6 miles each.

---

## Movement Rates

| Mode | Rate |
|------|------|
| On foot | 1 hex/day |
| Mounted (open terrain) | 2 hexes/day |
| Mounted (difficult terrain) | 1 hex/day or no benefit |

---

## Encounter Rolls

Roll d6 at the end of each hex traversed.

| Terrain | Encounter on |
|---------|-------------|
| Plains / road | 1 |
| Forest / hills | 1-2 |
| Mountains / swamp | 1-3 |
| Ruins / blighted land | 1-4 |

Apply a reaction roll to all encounters.

---

## Supplies and Rations

1 ration = 1 inventory slot = feeds 1 character for 7 days.

Running out of rations requires foraging (task roll, difficulty by terrain) or the party begins suffering conditions at the referee's discretion.

---

## Getting Lost

In difficult terrain without a guide or landmark, the referee may call for a navigation task roll. Failure means the party moves in an unintended direction; they may not know they are lost until they arrive somewhere unexpected.
