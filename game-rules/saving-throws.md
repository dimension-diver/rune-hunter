# Saving Throws

Roll d20 + relevant modifier vs the current target number. Meet or beat = success.

**Target: 15 - floor(level / 2).** Improves every two levels.

| Level | Target |
|-------|--------|
| 1-2 | 15 |
| 3-4 | 14 |
| 5-6 | 13 |
| 7-8 | 12 |
| 9-10 | 11 |

---

## Modifiers by Threat

| Threat | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Poison, disease, physical trauma | RES |
| Breath weapons, area effects | AGI |
| Beguilement, fear, illusion | CHA |
| Magical compulsion, death effects | RES or CHA (referee's call) |
| Fortune-dependent | LUC |
