# Rune Hunter. Player's Rules

---

## How Rolls Work

Three resolution systems, each for its own domain:

- **Combat**: d20 + ATK vs opponent DEF (attack) or d20 + DEF vs 10 + monster ATK (defense). Players always roll.
- **Saving throws**: d20 + relevant modifier vs 15 − floor(level ÷ 2).
- **Tasks**: d6, default 2-in-6, modified by ability scores and background.

All rolls are **player-facing**. Players roll for themselves, their hirelings, retainers, familiars, and summoned creatures. Monsters never roll.

---

## Character Creation

### 1. Roll ability scores
Roll 3d6 in order: **STR, AGI, RES, WIS, CHA, LUC**.

You may swap any two scores once after rolling. If the sum of all modifiers is −3 or worse, reroll the entire array.

| Score | Modifier |
|-------|----------|
| 3–6   | −1       |
| 7–14  | 0        |
| 15–18 | +1       |

### 2. Choose a genos
Kith or Chimera. Each gives one mechanical trait. See Kith and Chimera reference sheet.

### 3. Choose a class
**Hunter**, **Delver**, or **Caster** -- or one of their variants. Your class determines your combat chassis, HP die, and access to magic.

### 4. Starting HP
At level 1, take the maximum result on your class HD and add your RES modifier (minimum 1).

| Class | HD |
|-------|----|
| Hunter, Noble, Striker | d8 |
| Delver, Ranger, Seeker, Dabbler | d6 |
| Caster, Esper | d4 |

## Ability Scores

| Stat | Used for |
|------|----------|
| **Strength (STR)** | Melee attack rolls. Melee damage bonus (Hunters with Fighting Strength). Feats of physical power. |
| **Agility (AGI)** | Ranged attack rolls. DEF bonus when wearing light armor or unarmored. Stealth. Damage bonus (Delvers with Lethal Agility). |
| **Resilience (RES)** | HP modifier applied per die. Resistance to poison and disease. 0 RES is fatal. |
| **Wisdom (WIS)** | Spell research, languages, knowledge and lore tasks. Caps spells known. Runic Lore bonus slot (Casters and Seekers). |
| **Charm (CHA)** | Reaction rolls, hireling loyalty, social tasks. Saves vs beguilement, fear, illusions. Spirit Lore bonus slot (Espers). |
| **Luck (LUC)** | Die of fate modifier. Burnable for narrative outcomes. |

**STR modifier** adds to melee attack rolls for all classes.
**AGI modifier** adds to ranged attack rolls for all classes, and to DEF when wearing light armor or unarmored. Heavy armor negates the AGI modifier to DEF entirely.

## Classes

Choose one of the nine classes below. Each is self-contained -- the description tells you everything you need to play it. ATK, DEF, and spell slot progressions are in the tables that follow.

---

**Hunter:** Warriors who solve problems with steel. May divide their attack bonus across multiple targets in a single round -- a +4 Hunter can hit one target at +4 or four targets at +1 each. STR 15+ adds +1 to all melee damage. Heavy armor, any weapon.

**Noble:** Fights identically to a Hunter but can read and activate scrolls and attempt to use wands and rods on 1-3 in 6. STR 15+ no longer grants the melee damage bonus.

**Striker:** Wears no armor but gains +2 DEF from martial training; fists deal d6. Divides attack bonus across multiple unarmed targets like a Hunter. Casts runes as a Caster two levels lower, but only on self or delivered through a successful melee strike -- element or domain chosen at creation. WIS 15+ grants one extra Tier 1 slot per day. d8 HD.

**Delver:** Wears light armor and has a 2-in-6 chance to pick locks, move silently, find traps, and climb -- increasing to 3-in-6 at level 3, 4-in-6 at level 7, and 5-in-6 at level 10. Attacks from stealth deal ×2 damage with +4 to hit, increasing to ×3 at level 4, ×4 at level 7, and ×5 at level 10. Can read scrolls and attempt magic items. May move through an enemy zone without triggering a free attack when in light armor or unencumbered. AGI 15+ adds +1 damage with light and ranged weapons.

**Ranger:** As the Delver, but specialist skills cover wilderness survival, tracking, foraging, and calming animals instead of dungeon tools.

**Seeker:** Wears light armor, reads scrolls, identifies and crafts magic items. Casts runes as a Caster two levels lower -- no spell slots until level 3 unless WIS 15+. WIS 15+ grants one extra Tier 1 slot per day. Does not have the Delver's stealth damage bonus or AGI damage bonus.

**Caster:** Prepares spells from a spellbook each day using spell slots. Identifies and crafts magic items, reads scrolls. Wearing armor adds its AR value to enemy saves against the Caster's spells. WIS 15+ grants one extra Tier 1 slot per day. d4 HD.

**Esper:** Draws on spirits rather than runes -- CHA-based, wears light armor without penalty, knows wilderness skills (as Ranger). Cannot read scrolls or craft magic items; spirits are negotiated with, not studied. CHA 15+ grants one extra Tier 1 spirit invocation per day. d4 HD.

**Dabbler:** Self-taught rune user with full spell slots and scroll access, but cannot identify or craft magic items and gains no bonus from high WIS. d6 HD, light armor without penalty.

## Combat

### Round sequence
1. **Declare spells** (declare slot; Tier 1 resolves this round; higher tiers take multiple rounds; damage during casting loses spell and slot)
2. **Roll initiative** (each side rolls d6; winner acts first; ties simultaneous)
3. **Missiles** (ranged attacks in initiative order)
4. **Movement** (in initiative order)
5. **Melee** (in initiative order)
6. **Spells resolve** (in initiative order)

### Actions
- **Move 1 zone + take an action** (attack, use item, cast spell, task, aid)
- **Move 2 zones, no action**

### Attacking
**Melee:** d20 + ATK + STR vs opponent DEF

**Ranged:** d20 + ATK + AGI vs opponent DEF

**Defense:** when a monster attacks, the targeted player rolls d20 + DEF vs 10 + monster ATK. When a monster splits attacks, all targeted players roll simultaneously.

| Result | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Hit | Roll weapon damage, subtract AR, min 1 |
| Miss | No effect |
| Natural 20 on attack | Critical: bypass AR, reduce target AR by 1 for fight |
| Natural 20 on defense | Avoid hit and free counterattack this round |
| Natural 1 on defense | Monster scores a critical hit against you |

### DEF Modifiers
| Source | Modifier |
|--------|---------|
| Shield | +1 |
| Heavy shield | +2 |
| AGI modifier | +AGI mod (light armor or unarmored only; negated by heavy armor) |
| Striker unarmored | +2 (lost if any armor worn) |
| Magic items | As listed |

### Split Attack
Hunters (and variants that retain Split Attack) may divide their ATK bonus across multiple attacks in a single round, assigning at least +1 to each. Each attack must target a different enemy in range. Maximum attacks equals the ATK bonus. Magic weapon bonuses add to the budget. All attacks resolve in the same round.

*Example: ATK +4 may attack one enemy at +4, two at +2/+2, three at +2/+1/+1, or four at +1/+1/+1/+1.*

### Damage and AR
On a hit, roll weapon die + STR modifier. Subtract the target's AR. Minimum 1 damage.

**Critical hit (natural 20 on attack):** bypass AR entirely and reduce target's AR by 1 for this fight (minimum 0). PC armor degraded this way requires downtime repair.

### Weapons
| Option | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| One-handed | Standard damage die |
| Two-handed | Step up die (d4→d6, d6→d8) |
| Dual wield | +1 ATK; either weapon's die |
| Unarmed (Striker) | d6 |
| Shield | +1 DEF |
| Heavy shield | +2 DEF |

### Ranged combat
Ranged weapons cannot be used in melee. Cannot safely target enemies engaged in melee. Defenders cannot counterattack ranged attackers.

**Ammunition:** 3 bubbles. After each fight roll d6: on 4–6 mark one bubble. All 3 marked = one shot left. Resupply clears all. Rare ammo marks on 3+.

### Mounted combat
A mount grants +1 ATK and DEF in melee. A mounted charge (moving at least 1 zone before attacking) grants +2 ATK on the attack.

### Defeat
At 0 HP a character is **defeated**: not automatically dead. Referee determines consequences.

### Fleeing melee
A character who flees a melee zone gives opponents a free attack before leaving.

## Task Resolution

Default **2-in-6**. Roll d6 equal to or under target to succeed.

| Modifier | Chance |
|----------|--------|
| −1 | 1-in-6 |
| 0 | 2-in-6 |
| +1 | 3-in-6 |

Relevant genos or class adds +1. Skills improved through downtime or accomplishment.

## Inventory

10 slots: 2 hand slots and 2 body slots (included). Conditions occupy slots until removed. Heavy armor impedes stealth and swimming.

| Type | Removed by | Cost |
|------|-----------|------|
| Injury | Healer, surgeon, or rest | 1 treasure/slot |
| Disease | Herbalist or antidote | 1 treasure/slot |
| Curse / hex | Temple or ritual | 1–3 treasure/slot |
| Jinx | Spirit appeasement or quest | Narrative |
| Magical binding | Specific ritual | Narrative |

| Severity | Slots | Examples |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Minor | 1 | Sprained ankle, mild fever |
| Moderate | 2 | Broken bone, disease |
| Severe | 3 | Crippling wound, wasting sickness |
| Catastrophic | 4+ | Lost limb, death curse |

**Ability damage:** injury → 1d6 STR; poison → 1d6 RES (save or repeat daily); Jinx → 1d6 LUC. 0 in any stat = severe consequences. 0 RES = death.

## Luck

LUC modifies die of fate rolls. **Burning Luck:** spend 1 LUC permanently for a favorable outcome without rolling. Irreversible.

**Recovery:** 1d3 per week downtime up to starting score. No hard ceiling. 0 LUC = cosmically cursed until recovered.

---

## Advancement

No experience points. Advance through **expeditions** and **accomplishments**.

**Leveling up:** ATK, DEF, and save target advance automatically with level. HP: roll full HD pool, take new result if higher than current HP, minimum +1. RES modifier applies per die.

**Expedition rewards** (one per significant objective):

| Reward | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| +1 to a d6 task | Specific task or category |
| +1 to a stat | Referee determines or player makes a case |
| +1 to a save | Permanent, tied to what was survived |
| New talent | Requires fictional justification |

**Level bands:** advancement halts at band ceiling until an Accomplishment unlocks the next.

| Levels | Title | Scale |
|--------|-------|-------|
| 1–2 | Adventurer | Unknown. Survives on luck and grit. |
| 3–4 | Hero | Local reputation. |
| 5–6 | Lord | Regional power. |
| 7–8 | Legend | Continental scale. |
| 9–10 | Mythic | Near-godlike. |

**Accomplishments:** a deed of sufficient magnitude. Reward: unlock next band OR concrete bonus (+1 to a stat, +1 to all saves of a type, or significant talent). Advancing is always optional. ¹

## Spells

Casters, Seekers, and Strikers prepare spells from a spellbook after rest. Spells known capped by WIS score. Espers prepare spirit invocations through communion rather than study.

**Traditions:**
- **Mage:** WIS-based. Learns spells from books, inscriptions, and teachers.
- **Esper / Shaman:** CHA-based. Learns by communing with spirits. Cannot use scrolls or craft magic items.
- **Artificer:** Stores prepared spells in gadgets. Anyone activates; no slot cost to activator. Gadgets cannot be swapped mid-expedition.
