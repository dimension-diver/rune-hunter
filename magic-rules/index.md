# Magic Rules

Magic in this world is not fully understood. It predates the civilization being delved and the people who use it. Casters, Espers, and Seekers interact with it differently -- through scholarship, spirit, or accident -- but the underlying forces are the same.

This section is for magic-using characters. If you are playing a Hunter with no interest in spells, you do not need it.
