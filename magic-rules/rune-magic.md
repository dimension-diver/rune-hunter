# Rune Magic

Rune magic is the Mage and Artificer tradition. Spells are four-letter rune words -- BOLT, BURN, FIRE, RUIN -- drawn from inscriptions, teachers, and ancient archives. They are levelless: the same rune scales with MP investment up to the caster's current tier.

Rune invocations unlock qualitatively new expressions at higher MP costs, not just bigger numbers. BOLT at 1 MP hits one target; at 3 MP arcs to multiple targets; at 5 MP becomes a zone discharge. Higher invocations must be discovered or researched.

---

## Fire / Metal / Forge

| Rune | Tradition | Tier 1 Default | Notes |
|------|-----------|----------------|-------|
| FIRE | Universal | Strike one target for 1d6 fire damage. | Ignites a target or object. Scales from a spark to a conflagration engulfing a zone. |
| BURN | Universal | Target continues burning for 1 round after hit: takes 1 damage at start of their turn. | Sustained combustion: fire that spreads and lingers. Duration and area over burst damage. |
| SMOG | Universal | One target makes RES save or gains Poison condition (1 slot). | Toxic fumes. Scales to a persistent poisoned zone. |
| MELT | Mage | Destroy or significantly damage one non-magical object. | Dissolution by heat. Destroys objects, softens armor, collapses structures. |
| IRON | Universal | One weapon or piece of armor gains +1 to its relevant roll until next rest. | The forge rune. Hardens weapons and armor, transmutes materials. Shared across Fire and Earth. |
| WYRM | Mage + Shaman | Strike one target for 1d6: ignores AR 1 armor. | Draconic fire / void serpent. Mage: ancient destructive flame. Shaman: great serpent spirit communion. Shared rune: different fictional framing by tradition. |
| BOOM | Universal | One target takes 1d6 damage and is knocked back one zone. | Concussive explosion. Not fire per se but the force of ignition: knockback and impact. |

## Dark / Void / Arcane

| Rune | Tradition | Tier 1 Default | Notes |
|------|-----------|----------------|-------|
| VOID | Mage | One target takes 1d6 damage that bypasses all armor including magic. | Pure negation. Erases matter, magic, and effect. |
| FADE | Universal | Caster becomes invisible until they attack or cast. | Invisibility and ethereal movement. Pass through walls at high tiers. |
| NULL | Mage | End one active magical effect within range. | Antimagic. Dispel active spells, disrupt relic tech, counter other RUNE castings. |
| DOOM | Mage | One target is cursed: next time they fail a save they also gain a condition (1 slot). | Mark a target for death. Low tiers: a curse. High tiers: instant defeat regardless of HP. |
| DETH | Mage | One target makes RES save vs 16 or is immediately defeated regardless of HP. | The forbidden rune. Instant death effect. Most traditions do not teach this. |
| PALL | Mage | One zone is plunged into magical darkness: light sources don't work inside it. | Darkness descends. Magical shadow, blocks light, disorients and frightens. |
| DUSK | Universal | Caster can see perfectly in darkness for 1 exploration turn. | The threshold rune: neither light nor dark. Liminal rune: pairs with DAWN. |
| WANE | Mage | One target's next attack or spell does half effect. | Diminish and weaken. Reduce stats, drain MP, sap strength over time. |
| FEAR | Universal | One target makes CHA save or spends their next action fleeing. | Panic and rout. Does not affect undead or mindless creatures. |

## Meta Runes

Meta runes have no effect alone. They combine with other runes to alter or extend them.

| Rune | Tier 1 Default | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| WALL | No effect alone: must be combined with an element rune. | Creates a barrier. FIRE+WALL, RIME+WALL, VINE+WALL, etc. |
| RUNE | No effect alone: must specify a trigger condition and a second rune. | Inscribe another rune onto a surface for delayed or conditional activation. |
| ECHO | Repeat the last spell cast at its minimum (1 MP) effect. | Repeat the last rune cast for 1 MP regardless of source spell tier. |
| FLUX | No effect alone: must be combined with an active magical effect. | Amplify or alter an active magical effect combined with another rune. |
