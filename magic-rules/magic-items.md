# Magic Items

## Magic Weapons

Magic weapons add their bonus to both ATK rolls and damage. The ATK bonus also expands a Hunter's split attack budget.

| Bonus | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| +1 | Overcomes AR 1 reliably; one extra split target |
| +2 | Overcomes AR 2 reliably; two extra split targets |
| +3 | Overcomes all mundane AR; three extra split targets |

---

## Magic Armor

Raises AR beyond the mundane maximum of 2. Magic armor repairs itself between expeditions after crit degradation.

| AR | Source | Rarity |
|----|--------|--------|
| 3 | Magic +1 | Uncommon |
| 4 | Magic +2 | Rare |
| 5 | Magic +3 | Legendary |

---

## Relic Technology

Relic weapons (firearms, energy blades) bypass mundane AR. Magic AR still applies. Relics require power cells, which are scarce.

---

## Spells as Treasure

Finding a new rune or higher-tier invocation inscribed in an archive, held by a spirit willing to teach, or written in a forbidden tome is treasure equivalent to a magic item.

| Tier unlocked | Treasure value |
|---------------|----------------|
| Tier 1-2 | 2 |
| Tier 3-4 | 3-4 |
| Tier 5 | 5 |

---

## Buying & Selling

Magic items are not casual transactions. Finding a buyer or seller requires downtime, contacts, and often travel. Expect 50-75% of treasure value when selling; full value or above when buying. See [Downtime](../campaign-rules/downtime.md).

---

## Identifying Items

Casters and Seekers can identify magic items through the KNOW rune or a full rest spent examining the item. Nobles and Delvers may attempt identification on 1-3 in 6 using WIS modifier.

---

## Crafting

Casters with Magic Fluency can craft scrolls and magic items during downtime.

| Item | Cost | Time |
|------|------|------|
| Scroll (Tier 1-2) | 1-2 treasure | 1 week |
| Scroll (Tier 3+) | 3+ treasure | 1-2 weeks |
| Minor magic item | 3-5 treasure | 2-4 weeks |
| Significant magic item | 5-10 treasure | 1-3 months |

Crafting requires appropriate materials and a workspace. The referee may require specific components for unusual items.
