# Spirit Magic

Spirit magic is the Esper and Shaman tradition. Where Mages study runes as a system, Shamans negotiate with the spirits that animate the world -- the entities of water, wind, beast, and void that exist just behind the visible. Each invocation is not a spell so much as a request, and the spirits remember who asks and how.

Spirit invocations are learned through communion: finding a spirit willing to teach, surviving an encounter with one, or inheriting knowledge from another Shaman. They cannot be transcribed onto scrolls or stored in gadgets.

CHA governs spirit magic. CHA 15+ grants one extra Tier 1 spirit invocation slot per day.

## Water / Ice

| Rune | Tier 1 Default | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| TIDE | Push one target back one zone or knock them prone. | Flowing water: push, pull, current, wave. Force and movement. |
| WAVE | All targets in a zone make AGI save or are knocked prone. | A cresting surge. More aggressive than TIDE: impact and knockback. |
| RAIN | Zone becomes difficult terrain for 1d6 rounds. Extinguishes mundane fire. | Persistent precipitation. Slows enemies, extinguishes fire, creates difficult terrain. |
| FLOW | Redirect a body of water or create a current strong enough to push a person. | Shape and redirect water. Channel, dam, or release. |
| CURE | Remove one poison or disease condition from a target. | Removes poisons and toxins. Water purifying the body. Pairs with HEAL. |
| MIST | Create a zone of mist: all ranged attacks into or through it are at -1. | Universal rune: also accessible to Mages. |
| GILL | Caster can breathe and move normally underwater for 1 exploration turn. | Universal rune: also accessible to Mages. |

## Shared / Universal

These runes appear on both the Mage and Shaman lists. Either tradition can learn and cast them, though the fictional framing may differ: a Shaman calling on HEAL draws on the spirit of living things; a Mage casts the same rune through studied technique.

IRON, HEAL, MIST, GILL, SAND, DUST, GUST, RUSH, HUSH, LIFT, SLOW, GLOW, WARD, BANE, FADE, FEAR, LINK, SEND, CALM, STUN, LULL, PASS, RISE, LEAP, DASH, FIND, KNOW, SEEK, MARK
